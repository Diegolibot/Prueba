Chapter 6. Explanation


Nutrition

Nutrition is a transversal field that influences practically all the body's systems, so it could avoid frank deficiencies, mitigate risks to human health induced by space flight. Such as cognition, performance and morale, inadequate intake on cardiovascular performance, excessive nutrient intake, leading to excessive storage and increased oxidative stress, nutrient insufficiency, leading to bone loss, insufficient fruit and intake of vegetables on bone health, radiation protection and cardiovascular health.
